Hedy Lamarr, movie star and inventor

Hedy Lamarr often cast as a beauty but more interested in being a brain

Hedy had a life that is worth making a movie of.
From growing up in a Jewish environment at a time when Europe was in turmoil, meeting some of the main protagonists who will be some of the most terrible perpetrators of the atrocities, running away and becoming a movie star in Hollywood and playing alongside grandees such as Clark Gable and Spencer Tracy, to being involved in the war effort and being an accomplished visionary and inventor.
An extraordinary life which saw her marry 6 times and divorce almost as many times with the final wedding with her divorce lawyer also ending in divorce ironically...
She has been the inspiration for Catwoman and Snow White's looks but she was often frustrated by the beauty box she was put it and in her own words:

“Any girl can be glamorous. All you have to do is stand still and look stupid.”

She applied to join the inventor's council but was pushed back a few times either because she was a woman or more useful by using her beauty to raise funds for the war effort.

A beauty and a geek.

    Hedy's milestones:

  • 1914 Birth of Hedwig Eva Maria Kiesler November 9,in Vienna, Austria-Hungary
  • 1926 At the age of 12, she won a beauty contest in Vienna
  • 1930 start of acting career as an extra "Money on the street"
  • 1931 speaking part "Storm in a water glass"
  • 1932 lead role "No money needed"
  • 1933 lead role in "Ecstasy" and international fame
  • 1933 marries Friedrich Mandl, a rich Austrian industrialist
  • 1937 runs away to London and changes name to Hedy Lamarr
  • 1938 arrives in the USA and first movie lead in the Hollywood movie "Algiers"
  • 1939 marries Gene Markey, an American screenwriter and producer
  • 1940's Fundraising for the war effort
  • 1941 divorces Gene Markey
  • 1942 U.S. Patent 2292387 for the Secret Communication System using Frequency Hopping (with George Antheil)
    The patent paving the way for future GPS, WIFI and Bluetooth technologies
  • 1943 marries John Loder, a British actor with whom she will have 2 children + 1...
  • 1947 divorces John Loder
  • 1951 stars in "Samson and Delilah"
  • 1953 becomes US citizen
  • 1958 her last movie "The female animal"
  • 1960 gets a star on the Hollywood walk of fame
  • 1997 awarded with George Antheil the Electronic Frontier Foundation Pioneer Award https://www.eff.org/
  • 1997 awarded the BULBIE Gnass Spirit of Achievement Award "the oscars of inventing"
  • 2000 death at 85 years old at her home near Orlando, Florida, USA.
